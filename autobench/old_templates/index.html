Welcome to Autobench!

  • Autobench is a tool developed by the Storage Solutions Engineering team at Micron.
  • Autobench is an inventory management and test automation utility. Here are a few of the things you can do:
Click the "My Info" tab to see information about yourself, and projects that you are associated with.
Hover over or click the "Inventory" tab to see servers and projects that you can access.
Hover over the "Actions" tab to see what actions you can perform.
Click the "Jobs" tab to see jobs that you've scheduled.
Most tables are searchable and sortable. You can filter search results by typing in the filter box, or sort by clicking on a column header.
